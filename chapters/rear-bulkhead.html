Rear Bulkhead

Contents / Rear Bulkhead

Another task that takes time and patience to acheive a good fit.

  1. Using card cut out the profile of the outside edge using the floor as the starting point, cut small bits and get it perfect.
  2. Mark a line off the top of the card onto the chassis (white board marker)
  3. Repeat this profile cut for the otherside.
  4. Measure between the 2 marks on the chassis
  5. Lay the cad profiles on the bulk head material with the lower edge on the bottom of the material, and the 2 marks the distance apart you measured.
  6. Draw round the card profiles and discard.
  7. Create a card profile for the drivers side of the transmission tunnel
  8. We will be making 2 pieces to aid fitting. This profile should have a cut out above the top of the tunnel towards the drivers side to allow the 2nd peice to fasten to the box section.
  9. Mark this peice as before, measure distance, place on material, draw round and discard.
  10. Repeat the profile for the passenger side. Many more angle and lines to work with here.
  11. Minimise the cut out required for the handbrake cables.
  12. The Whole piece should now be drawn, cut using a sharp knife and steel ruler.
  13. Fitting should involve bending the piece in to place.
  14. Fasten with 2.5mm drill & 3mm black screws.
