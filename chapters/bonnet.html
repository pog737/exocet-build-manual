Bonnet

Contents / Bonnet
  1. Add masking tape to the chassis rails where bonnet will sit for protection.
  2. Sit bonnet in place ensuring centered and as far fowrard as possible.
  3. Apply pressure where catches will sit and check the gaps between chassis and panel
  4. If the gaps are uneven, mark the high bits to be removed. Remember to check that you are not going to low to impact the motor.
  5. Remove & file the panel as marked.
  6. Refit and repeat adjustment process until gaps are even.
  7. It may be useful to cut a small arc out of the cockpit edge of the bonnet where the chassis rails sit. this will aid with locating.
  8. Check panel is level left to right, remove and adjust full length if required.
  9. Fit edging strip on sides and cockpit edge of bonnet.
  10. Mark and drill/cut holes for bonnet catches depending on the type you are using.
  11. Set tension on catches for a firm fit.

Remember the tension on the Bonnet catches themselves will deform the bonnet slightly to make a pressure fit. This will affect gaps.
