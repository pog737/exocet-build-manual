The NA we used had 2 horns, we opted to keep both.

  1. On the front cross rail, ensuring enough clearance from the nose cone, mark the first hole.
  2. Aprx 200mm away mark the 2nd.
  4. Drill (9.2mm) and rivnut (m6) these marks.
  5. Fit horns facing forwards/towards each other, one will need to have the earth connector in the bolt.
  6. Connect to the wiring (2 single spade connectors near the cooling fan connector)
