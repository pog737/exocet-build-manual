Side Panel Fitting

Contents / Side Panel Fitting
  1. Clamp the Panel back in place, ensuring protective cover is outside.
  2. Starting at the front, mark 10mm in from the edge and drill a 2.5mm hole top and bottom of front edge.
  3. Fasten in place with the black 10mm screws.
  4. Mark, drill and screw one more central to these 2 screws.
  5. Mark all the way along the bottom edge at 150mm centers, 10mm up.
  6. Keep tension on the panel, ensuring its high enough, drill 2 holes at a time and fit screws.
  7. Repeat previous until all bottom screws are done.
  8. Ensure you have a screw to keep the corner in place.
  9. Mark, drill and screw top screw.
  10. Mark, drill and screw one more central to these 2 screws.
  11. Repaeat for the other side.
