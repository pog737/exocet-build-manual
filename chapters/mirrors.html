Mirrors

Contents / Mirrors

There are 2 options here, either mount to the top chassis rail, or to the bonnet. We chose the bonnet.

  1. Mark both sides of the bonnet in the small flat (ish) area, ensuring clearance for wind deflector.
  2. mark the center of the bonnet, again ensuring the clearance.
  3. Drill using a stepper drill to the required 8(?)mm size
  4. Fit the mirrors with another tightness not to slip but can be moved.
  5. Site in the car, adjust the angles
  6. Tightent the locking nut from the top of the mirror to hold them in place.
